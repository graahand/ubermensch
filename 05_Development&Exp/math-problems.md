# MATHS BENCHMARK QUESTIONS (CASUAL BENCHMARK)

## Basic Arithmetic

What is 123 + 456?
What is 1000 - 789?
What is 12 × 13?
What is 144 ÷ 12?

## Algebra

Solve for x: 2x + 3 = 7.
Simplify the expression: 3(x + 2) - 2(x - 1).
Factor the quadratic expression: x² - 5x + 6.
Solve the system of equations:
2x + y = 8
x - y = 1

## Geometry

What is the area of a rectangle with length 5 and width 3?
What is the volume of a cube with side length 4?
If a right triangle has legs of length 3 and 4, what is the length of the hypotenuse?
What is the circumference of a circle with radius 7?


## Calculus

What is the derivative of f(x) = x² with respect to x?

## Trigonometry

What is the period of the function y = sin(2x)?
